Commitment Quality

Jenga Taifa’s engineering organization is ISO 9001:2015 certified across all engineering markets. This certification reinforces our commitment to quality, operational excellence and continuous improvement, and reflects our adherence to the highest standards of quality management.

Customized Project solutions

With the flexibility to handle diverse project sizes and scopes, we leverage our extensive experience, knowledge and specialized teams to effectively plan, manage and execute projects of any scale.

Full Transparency

For complex and alternative delivery models to succeed, all team members must be on the same page — all partners working together from the same data points and keeping an open dialogue to resolve issues throughout the project lifecycle.

Integrated Quantity Management

Jenga Taifa's engineering teams focus on optimizing quantities and cost efficiency on projects of all sizes. Through regular reviews of project quantities, we ensure that construction remains as economical as possible. This meticulous approach reduces construction hours in the field, improving safety and keeping total installed costs down for our owners.

Data Driven Outcome Certainity

Jenga Taifa's project management system drives informed decision-making from estimate to completion. With cloud-based solutions connecting project data across scope, cost and schedules, clients gain greater control, cost management, and confidence for successful project outcomes.
