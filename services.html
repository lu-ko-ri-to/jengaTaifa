Services

At Jenga Taifa Solutions we offer a variety of services that include:

General Construction

We provide proffesional expertise in residential construction,commercial construction and industrial construction

Renovation and remodeling

Jenga Taifa Solutions offers a masterpiece in Home and office renovations,kitchen and bathroom remodelling and structural repairs and upgrades.Our recent mega project was the renovation of the Nairobi Statehouse early in the year

Civil Engineering and infrastructre

We do not miss out in the civil engineering sector as we do road construction and paving,bridges and highway and finally drainage and sewer systems

Architectural and Design services

With a great team of qualified architects we also offer architectural services that include 3D modelling and visualization,Blueprint and Plan development and interior and exterior designing.

Project Management

Developing and ensuring adherence to the highest business standards possible and providing oversight to corporate structure, safety, ethics, compliance, and compensation. Our Board includes world-class, independent directors whose extensive expertise enables us to support our employees, clients, and shareholders. We are proud of the diversity of our directors’ backgrounds and experience. The Board embodies our Core Values and provides oversight and guidance to promote a strong ethical standard and a unified “One Company” approach in everything we do.

Roofing and waterproofing

We also do roof installation and repair as well as waterproofing and insulation solutions

Engineering

JengaNchi 3,600+ engineers and designers are dedicated to providing our clients with collaborative designs, efficient project execution, technical compliance and constructability expertise during each phase of the project lifecycle. By regularly rotating through field operations, our engineers gain practical, full-lifecycle project insights to deliver unmatched, construction-focused solutions.

JengaNchi brings a diverse track record for successful execution of complex design-build and EPC projects across North America, including:

  • 25+ years of direct, design engineering project execution
  • Over $150 billion in design-build and EPC projects hence the name multibillion app
  • Design and engineering expertise across five major markets

Streamlined Construction

We leverage our construction roots to identify opportunities for simplification, prefabrication and modularization, optimizing construction schedules and minimizing disruptions.

Technology Intergration

We understand and cater to our owners’ unique technology packages — licensed or proprietary — to ensure optimized configuration and seamless execution.

Improved cost control

Our construction-focused engineering approach helps anticipate and address potential cost overruns, which leads to more accurate estimates and, ultimately, more certain project outcomes.

Commitment Quality

Jenga Taifa’s engineering organization is ISO 9001:2015 certified across all engineering markets. This certification reinforces our commitment to quality, operational excellence and continuous improvement, and reflects our adherence to the highest standards of quality management.

Customized Project solutions

With the flexibility to handle diverse project sizes and scopes, we leverage our extensive experience, knowledge and specialized teams to effectively plan, manage and execute projects of any scale.

Full Transparency

For complex and alternative delivery models to succeed, all team members must be on the same page — all partners working together from the same data points and keeping an open dialogue to resolve issues throughout the project lifecycle.

Integrated Quantity Management

Jenga Taifa's engineering teams focus on optimizing quantities and cost efficiency on projects of all sizes. Through regular reviews of project quantities, we ensure that construction remains as economical as possible. This meticulous approach reduces construction hours in the field, improving safety and keeping total installed costs down for our owners.

Data Driven Outcome Certainity

Jenga Taifa's project management system drives informed decision-making from estimate to completion. With cloud-based solutions connecting project data across scope, cost and schedules, clients gain greater control, cost management, and confidence for successful project outcomes.
